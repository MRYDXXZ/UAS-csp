The Beginning Of The Business
January 20, 2025

Feran's Bracelets is a small home-based business that began in early 2022. The business was initiated by Inez, a highly creative and diligent woman with a talent for crafting unique handmade accessories. Initially, Inez started by casually making bracelets from beads she was given. With perseverance and a strong passion for creativity, she began experimenting with various designs and materials, creating bracelets that were not only beautiful but also unique. Over time, Inez came up with the idea to sell her creations, and that’s how Feran's Bracelets was born.

Vision and Mission
December 15, 2023

The vision of Feran's Bracelets is to become one of the leading handmade bracelet brands known for the quality and uniqueness of its products. Our mission is: 1.Creating High-Quality Products: Using the finest materials to ensure that every bracelet produced is both durable and beautiful. 2.Empowering Creativity: Providing a space for Inez and other local artisans to express their creativity through handmade crafts. 3.Supporting the Community: Contributing to the local economy by employing local artisans and sourcing sustainable raw materials.

Marketing and Reach
March 10, 2022

Inez decided to market her products through online platforms like Instagram, where she could reach a wider audience. By leveraging social media, Inez is able to: Showcase Creations: Upload high-quality and appealing photos of her products to capture the attention of potential buyers. Stay on Trend: Keep up with the latest trends in fashion and handmade crafts to continuously innovate and create relevant products.
